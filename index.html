What is the UHDC?

The Unified Help Desk Console (UHDC) is an advanced, centralized dashboard built to put the most powerful diagnostic and remediation tools behind a single pane of glass. It is designed specifically to accelerate live phone support and drastically reduce the time users spend on hold.

Instead of navigating slow web portals or walking frustrated users through complex command-line steps, technicians can instantly map users to their physical hardware, retrieve BitLocker keys, reset corrupted browser profiles, and push software deployments with a single button click.
